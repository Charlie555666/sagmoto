Frequently Asked Questions

What makes the Off-Road 4×4 Series different from standard trucks?

The Off-Road 4×4 Series features all-wheel drive with locking differentials, a transfer case with low-range gearing, significantly higher ground clearance, reinforced military-grade chassis, specialized off-road tires, and a snorkel air intake for deep water fording.

What drive configurations are available for off-road models?

SAGMOTO offers 4×4, 6×6, and 8×8 off-road configurations. The 4×4 is suitable for construction and oil field support, 6×6 for heavy off-road logistics and military transport, and 8×8 for extreme heavy-duty off-road applications including mobile cranes and missile systems.

Can the off-road trucks be used for military applications?

Yes. SAGMOTO's off-road platform is adapted for military logistics, troop transport, and equipment carrier roles. Military variants feature blackout lighting, NATO towing compatibility, weapon mounts compatibility, and enhanced EMP protection on request. Export licenses may apply.

What is the water fording depth?

The Off-Road 4×4 Series supports water fording depths of up to 1.2 meters with the standard snorkel intake. Extended fording kits are available for depths up to 1.8 meters for specialized applications. All critical electronics are sealed and protected.

What after-sales support is available in remote locations?

Shaanxi Fenghan Trading provides expeditionary support packages for remote operations including extended spare parts kits, field service manuals, remote diagnostic support, and train-the-trainer programs for local maintenance teams. Contact us for customized support plans.

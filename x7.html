Frequently Asked Questions

What makes the X7 Series ideal for urban logistics?

The X7 Series features a compact turning radius, lightweight chassis design (800×232mm frame), and fuel-efficient engines (130-210 HP). Its maneuverability and low operating cost make it perfect for city deliveries and short-haul transport.

What engine is in the X7?

The X7 Series is powered by Yuchai engines across three tiers: YC4FA (130-160 HP) for light-duty, YC4D (160-180 HP) for balanced performance, and YC4E (180-210 HP) for maximum capacity. All engines meet EURO II through EURO V emission standards.

What is the fuel tank capacity of the X7?

The X7 comes with a 90L-150L fuel tank depending on configuration. The compact fuel capacity is optimized for urban and regional routes where refueling infrastructure is readily available, keeping chassis weight down for higher payload.

Does the X7 support 4×4 drive?

Yes, select X7 variants offer 4×4 all-wheel drive configuration, making them suitable for unpaved roads, agricultural applications, and light off-road use. The 4×4 variants feature a transfer case and reinforced front axle.

How does the X7 compare to the X9 in terms of capacity?

The X7 is positioned below the X9 in the SAGMOTO lineup. The X7 focuses on light-to-medium duty with GVWR up to 25T, while the X9 extends to heavier configurations up to 45T (GCW) with larger cabs and more powerful engine options.

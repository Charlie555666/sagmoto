Delivery & Logistics to Central Asia

Road Transport: Trucks are driven or flatbed-transported via the Khorgos/Dostyk border crossing into Kazakhstan, then onwards to Kyrgyzstan, Uzbekistan, or Tajikistan. The Torugart and Irkeshtam passes connect directly to Kyrgyzstan.

Rail Freight: Container or open wagon rail transport from Xi'an to Almaty (Kazakhstan), Tashkent (Uzbekistan), or Dushanbe (Tajikistan). Rail is cost-effective for bulk orders of 5+ units.

Delivery Timeline: 15-25 days transit time depending on destination. Customs clearance normally takes 3-5 working days with proper documentation.

Insurance & Documentation: Full cargo insurance included. Export documentation package: commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin (Form E for CIS preferences), bill of lading, and technical passport in Russian.

X6 / X6s Dump Truck — Medium-Duty Versatility

The SAGMOTO X6 and X6s are medium-duty dump trucks designed for small to medium-scale mining, urban construction, and infrastructure projects. Available in 4x2 (12T payload) and 6x4 (18T payload) configurations with Cummins ISD, ISF, and D6.7EVIE engines from 220HP to 320HP. A popular choice in Tajikistan, Azerbaijan, and Mexico markets where a balance of capability and cost-effectiveness is required. The X6s variant adds a semi-high roof cab for improved driver comfort.

X6 Dump Truck FAQ

Q: Is the X6 dump truck suitable for Central Asian markets?

Absolutely. The X6 has been specifically exported to Tajikistan (both 4x2 and 6x4 variants) and Azerbaijan. The 220HP Weichai engine with 8-speed manual transmission is proven reliable in Central Asian mining conditions.

Q: X6 vs X6s - what is the difference?

The X6s features a semi-high roof cab (2220mm width) with improved visibility and comfort. The X6 uses a standard flat roof cab. Both share the same engine, transmission, and chassis options.

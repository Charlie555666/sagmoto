Why Choose E3 Dump Truck

Same Powertrain, Lower Cost

Same Cummins ISME engine and FAST transmission as the X3s, but with simplified cab interior and trim. Saves RMB 7,000-56,000 per unit without compromising on reliability.

Right-Hand Drive Available

E3 is our most popular RHD dump truck platform. Exported to Zambia, Indonesia, and other left-hand traffic countries. Same quality, mirrored controls.

Africa Market Proven

Successful deployments in Angola quarries, Zambia copper mines, and Cote dIvoire infrastructure projects. Handles African road conditions and climate reliably.

AMT Option Available

The 440HP E3 6x4 offers a F13JZ24A AMT (Automated Manual Transmission) option, providing easier operation for less experienced drivers.

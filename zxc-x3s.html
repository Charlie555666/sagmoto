X3s Dump Truck FAQ

Q: What makes the X3s suitable for high-altitude mining?

The Cummins ISME engine with Jacobs brake maintains power at high altitude. We recommend the 420HP version for altitudes above 3,000m. This configuration is successfully used in Tajikistan and Ecuador mountain mines.

Q: 6x4 vs 8x4 - which should I choose?

6x4: 50T GVWR, 4.8-5.8m body, more maneuverable, lower fuel consumption. Best for general mining and construction. 8x4: 60T GVWR, 6.2-7.4m body, higher payload per trip. Best for high-volume earthwork.

Q: What after-sales support do you provide?

We provide 12-month OEM warranty, spare parts catalog, Russian/English/French manuals, WhatsApp technical support within 15 minutes, and can arrange spare parts stock at your location. Service network covering Central Asia, Africa, and Latin America.
